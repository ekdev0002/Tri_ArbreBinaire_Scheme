Information
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sorting of a binary research tree, using the functional paradigm. This   is  the 
sorting of an integer list with the concept of a three-part binary search tree, 
namely, prefixed sorting, infixed sorting and postfixed sorting.
     * Enter a list of integers: 6 4 5 2 1 3

Running
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Unzip the Run.zip and launch the Run.exe
